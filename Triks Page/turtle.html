Easy Step by Step Instructions: Turtle

Step One:

Start with the white side of the paper up. Fold in half vertically and unfold. Then, turn the paper over.

Step Two:

Fold the top edges to the centerline. Then, turn the paper over.

Step Three:

Fold the top edges to the centerline again, but only through the top layer. Be careful not to crease the flaps you made in the previous step (see picture).

Step Four:

Fold the bottom edges up to meet the centerline. One small white triangle will sit on top of the other.

Step Five:

Fold the right flap down, aligning the reference point with the bottom edge.

Step Six:

Repeat on the left side.

Step Seven:

Fold the top square area in half along the horizontal diagonal.

Step Eight:

Fold the top flap up, aligning the circled points.

Step Nine:

Fold the left and right edges of the top flap up to meet the top edge. Then, unfold.

Step Ten:

Swivel fold both the left and right sides inward toward the center, using the creases from the previous step as guides.

Step Eleven:

Pleat the bottom corner to form the turtle's tail. Then, turn the model over.

Step Twelve:

Pleat the tail and narrow the body.

Step Thirteen:

Narrow the arms and legs to add shape and volume.

Tip: Pleat the arms to shorten them and give more volume to your model.

Tadaa!! Your origami turtle is completed.

NOTE:If you are a beginner, then use Kami paper to make origami. While for experts, You can use Origamido paper, Tant paper or double tissue paper
